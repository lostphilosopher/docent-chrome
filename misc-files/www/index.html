ABOUT DOCENT

Company Overview

DocentEDU helps teachers and students by allowing them to create meaningful and helpful layers on top of the available online contents saving time, increasing focus, and streamlining workflow efficiency.

Product

Our solution helps teachers and students by providing layered contents, questions, discussion and expertise all together in one place. This saves focus, time, and efficiency for the teachers and provides unlimited learning possibilities for students.

Target Audience

Initially for freemium version the main target audience for DocentEDU is teachers and students but later for premium version they will also work with school districts, educational institutions and other businesses who require layered access to digital contents.



THE VISION



Students loose focus and engagement with every separation while working on an assignment.

  • Analog Separation - textbooks and worksheets
  • Digital Separation - digital content and digital worksheets
  • The Layered Approach - Integrating content and engagement

Docent gives students and teachers the Layered approach to any website. This increases efficiency and simplifies the workflow by combining the content, questions, discussion, and expert layers.

PRICING


Freemium to Use Today

Premium to Grow Tomorrow

We want to get DocentEDU into the hands of as many educators and students as we possibly can because we know it can have such a big impact on education today. WE believe that the most efficient way to do this is through a freemium model. The free version would support smaller classrooms and would provide all of the basic functionality we showed you today.

In the future, we want to bring even more functionality… like:

  • Allowing students and educators to capture video of themselves and embedding videos right on the page
  • We would love to build integrations into popular LMS like Moodle and Blackboard.
  • Our ultimate vision: is an easy method for crowdsourced learning templates.

FAQ


Q: Why is this a problem? Aren’t there other “web annotation” tools out there?
A: DocentEDU is not just another web annotation tool. DocentEDU layers engaging content and social interaction on top of existing static online content. It brings together content, guide, and discussion in layers on top of the content into an effective workflow that leads to more focus. Other tools out there don’t come close to this model.

Q: Is this for teachers or students?
A: This is for as many teachers and students as possible. We believe that this is a tool for teaching and learning, and that each individual teacher and student will bring their own personal ideas to DocentEDU to transform their education.

Q: What cool features might you roll out in the future?
A: The sky’s the limit! DocentEDU is in beta, and wants our customers to live in beta as well. We are open to ideas and have some moonshot ideas as well.
